Additional Info:

1) This service operates on the honor system. There's no way we can confirm who you are, where you live, or which candidate you ultimately vote for.

2) This service is designed to help Hillary Clinton in swing states. It intends to serve as a good-faith secondary option to Third Party supporters who can't stomach indirectly helping Donald Trump.

3) We believe that vote trading is legal because:

- voting is political speech
- vote trading is a form of solidarity
- the actual vote trade is fundamentally unverifiable and unenforceable
- vote traders receive no consideration or inducement

FAQ

Won't Trump voters rig the system?

Fundamentally, a vote swap has the effect of 1) helping Hillary Clinton in a swing state, 2) helping a Third Party in a safe state. While false entries do potentially erode trust in the process, they do not alter the basic dynamic of the system.

Is vote swapping actually effective?

It depends on adoption and trust in the system. In 2000, "Nader Trader" websites gained significant attention and adoption, and presumably had some effect in swaying Third Party voters in critical states.
